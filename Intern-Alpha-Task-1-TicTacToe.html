📋 Game Rules

1️⃣ Players Alternate

Players take turns placing their symbol (Tablet or Capsule) on the 3x3 grid.

2️⃣ Get Three in a Row

Win by aligning 3 of your symbols horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

3️⃣ Tie or Win

If all 9 spaces are filled with no winner, it's a tie game.
